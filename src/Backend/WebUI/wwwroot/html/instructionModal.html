How to use Dream Travel :)

❮
1 / 7
Type in the list of locations you would love to visit.
They will be displayed on the map.
2 / 7
If you enable the 'Optimize Road' option
order of inserted locations may be changed!
First and last locations always remains the same.
3 / 7
Press 'Apply' button - Dream Travel will calculate the optimal route.
4 / 7
All the details regarding your travel are displayed on the top of the page.
5 / 7
You can apply changes for your travel.
Ex: drag and drop markers on the map to change the locations or disable 'Optimize road' option.
6 / 7
You can play with the limit of the toll fee. In general - more money you spent, faster the travel goes.
7 / 7
Final remarks:
• Use only english or native names of the locations
• You can include from 2 to 30 locations in your travel
• All the cost are displayed in Euro currency
• Travel time is displayed in HH:mm:ss format
• Dream Travel supports Google Maps features - street view, satellite map...
